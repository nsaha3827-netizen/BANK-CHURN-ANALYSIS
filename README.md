# BANK-CHURN-ANALYSIS
A data-driven analysis of customer churn in the banking sector using Excel.
The goal is to identify key factors responsible for customer attrition and provide actionable insights to help improve customer retention and decision-making.

#Detail	Description:
Source	Customer Churn Records (25 rows)
Format	CSV
Features	Credit score, demographics, account balance, tenure, estimated salary, and churn status

Tools & Techniques Used:
Excel
      Data cleaning
      Pivot tables
      Data visualization (charts)
      Insight extraction
Power point & AI
      To make presentation

#Project Workflow
1.Data import and cleaning
2.Handling missing / inconsistent values
3.Exploratory data analysis (EDA)
4.Customer churn segmentation
5.Insights and action recommendations

#Key Insights:
1.Customers with low credit scores showed a significantly higher churn rate.

2.Inactive customers and those with low engagement were more likely to leave.

3.Senior citizens and single account holders had higher churn probability.

4.Customers with lower account balance were more likely to churn than high-balance users.

5.Improving customer engagement and targeted retention strategy can reduce churn.
